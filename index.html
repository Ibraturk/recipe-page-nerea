Stuffed Croissant

drag_indicator

Prepare this wonderful recipe for the stuffed croissant. The perfect combination for brunch with the spicy touch of the vermouth, the freshness of the pear and arugula accompanied by the best Roquefort cheese.

YIELDS

1 Serving

PREEP TIME

15 minutes

COOCK TIME

10 minutes

TOTAL TIME

35 minutes

Ingredients

Stuffed

Instructions

1
Cut in the middle the croissant and put in the oven with the muzzarella cheese
2
Make thin slices of the ham and also cut the pear into slices
3
Take the croissant out of the oven and place a bed of arugula as a base
4
Continue with the Iberian ham, pears and pieces of Roquefort cheese. Finish by placing the spicy vermouth dressing and you are ready to enjoy this dish.
